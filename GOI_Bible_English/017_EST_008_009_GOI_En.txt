At that time, the king's scribes were summoned in the third month, which is the month of Sivan, on the twenty-third day thereof; and it was written according to all that Mordecai commanded unto the Jews and unto the lieutenants and governors and rulers of the provinces from India even unto Cush, one hundred twenty-seven provinces, each province according to its writing and each people according to their language, and unto the Jews according to their writing and according to their language.
