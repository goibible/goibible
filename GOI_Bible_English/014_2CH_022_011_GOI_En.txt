Jehoshabeath daughter of the king took Joash son of Ahaziah and stole him from among the children of the king who were being murdered, and she put him and his nurse in the chamber of the beds, and Jehoshabeath daughter of the king Jehoram wife of Jehoiada the priest hid him, for she was sister of Ahaziah, because of Athaliah, and she did not let him be killed.
