And after this saith the LORD I will give Zedekiah king of Judah and his servants and the people and those left in this city from the pestilence from the sword and from the famine into the hand of Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon and into the hand of their enemies and into the hand of those who seek their lives and he will strike them by the sword he will not pity them nor have compassion nor show mercy.
