But these you shall not eat among those that bring up the cud and have a divided hoof: the camel, because it brings up the cud but does not have a divided hoof, it is unclean to you; the hare, because it brings up the cud but does not have a divided hoof, it is unclean to you; the coney, because it brings up the cud but does not have a divided hoof, it is unclean to you.
